Timm Dauth is a german photo- and videographer based in Munich, Germany. He has its roots in BMX Culture where his passion for video and photo was born from. He is mainly capturing the culture of skateboarding and BMX but is also indulging in Fashion and journalism work. For contact information, please visit the "contact" page.
